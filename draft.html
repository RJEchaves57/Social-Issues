How AI Used to Spread Misinformation?

01 - Definition

02 - Philippine Context

1

Deepfake

AI-powered misinformation includes deepfakes, AI-generated text, automated bot networks that spread fake news, and misleading contextualization of truthful content. These tools can make fabricated content.

2

Philippine Context

In July–August of this year a number of digitally manipulated political videos circulated, confusing and stirring social conflict. The rapid integration of AI into media content creation has revolutionized dissemination but also generated convincing misinformation such as deepfakes, posing significant risks to democratic processes.

3

Impact

The impact of AI misinformation is a pressing concern, but some experts argue the concerns are overblown. When AI can generate convincing misinformation, research suggests that consumption of misinformation is often limited by demand rather than supply—availability alone may not dramatically increase spread..
